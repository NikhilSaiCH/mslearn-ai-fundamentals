## Task 2: Cleaning the Clustering Dataset

In this task,

1. Select **Designer (1)** from the left navigation menu, then under the **New pipeline** section (Classic prebuilt), click **+ Create a new pipeline using classic prebuilt components (2)**.

    ![](../images/L13-T02-01.png)

1. A pipeline designer panel will open. To update the name of the pipeline, click on **pencil (1)** icon, provide draft name as **SC_Clustering (2)** and draft description as **SC_Clustering (3)**. Then, click on **Save (4)**.

    ![](../images/L13-T02-02.png)

1. Under the **Data (1)** tab, click on **+ (2)** icon.

    ![](../images/L13-T02-03.png)

1. Provide data asset name as **SC_Clustering (1)** and click on **Next (2)**.

    ![](../images/L13-T02-04.png)

1. Choose **From local files (1)** option for data source and click on **Next (2)**.

    ![](../images/L13-T02-05.png)

1. Select **workspaceblobstore (1)** for data store and click on **Next (2)**.

    ![](../images/L13-T02-06.png)

1. Next, click on **Upload files or folder (1)** and select **Upload files (2)**.

    ![](../images/L13-T02-07.png)

1. Navigate to **C:\AllFiles\Unit 1-Lesson 13 - Cleaning Traffic Datasets in Azure ML Designer (1)** folder, select **SC_Clustering (2)** CSV file and click on **Open (3)**.

    ![](../images/L13-T02-08.png)

1. After uploading file, make sure that **SC_Clustering.csv (1)** file is visible under upload list. Then click on **Next (2)**.

    ![](../images/L13-T02-09.png)

1. Check the preview of SC_Clustering.csv file and click on **Next**.

    ![](../images/L13-T02-10.png)

1. Review the schema of SC_Clustering.csv file and click on **Next**.

    ![](../images/L13-T02-11.png)

1. Review the settings done till now for data asset and click on **Create**.

    ![](../images/L13-T02-12.png)

1. Under the **Data** tab, find your dataset **SC_Clustering** and drag it onto the pipeline canvas to use it as the starting point of your machine learning workflow.
